Research Interests ​

  • 🌬️ Wind-induced Vibration Control of Structures
    Focus on mitigating the effects of wind-induced vibrations on large-scale structures.

  • ⚡ Vibration Energy Harvesting
    Exploring innovative methods for harvesting energy from structural vibrations.

  • 🔧 Nonlinear Vibration Control
    Developing advanced techniques related to nonlinear energy sink inerter to control nonlinear vibrations in complex systems.

  • 💥 Impact Dynamics Behavior and Protection of Structures
    Studying impact dynamics and developing protection mechanisms for structural safety.
